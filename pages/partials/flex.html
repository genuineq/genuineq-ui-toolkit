Flex

The flex property is a sub-property of the Flexible Box Layout module.

HTML

<div class="d-flex justify-content-space-around align-items-space-around flex-wrap-wrap">
  <div class="height-2 width-1 background-color-info"></div>
  <div class="height-2 width-1 background-color-info"></div>
  <div class="height-2 width-1 background-color-info"></div>
  <div class="height-2 width-1 background-color-info"></div>
  <div class="height-2 width-1 background-color-info"></div>
  <div class="height-2 width-1 background-color-info"></div>
  <div class="height-2 width-1 background-color-info"></div>
</div>
