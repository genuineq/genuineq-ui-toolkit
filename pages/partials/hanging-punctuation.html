Hanging-Punctuation

The hanging-punctuation property aims at giving web designers a finer grained control over typography on the web.

“Let your tears flow, but let them also cease. Let deepest sighs be drawn from your breast, but let them also find an end.” — Seneca

HTML

<blockquote class="hanging-punctuation-first">
  “Let your tears flow, but let them also cease. Let deepest sighs be drawn from your breast, but let them also find an end.” — Seneca
</blockquote>

SCSS

$hanging-punctuation: ();
$hanging-punctuation: map-merge(
  (
    "none": none,
    "first": first,
    "last": last,
    "force-end": force-end,
    "allow-end": allow-end,
  ),
  $hanging-punctuation
);
