Visibility

The visibility property in CSS has two different functions. It hides rows and columns of a table, and it also hides an element without changing the layout.

Hi, I'm hidden. Notice that all of my styling is hidden as well, and that I still take up space, even though you can't see me.
Howdy, my parent element is hidden, but I'm still visible.
Hover over me to make my parent visible.

HTML

<div class="visibility-hidden background-color-info border-style-dotted border-color-danger border-width-5 p-4 visibility-example">
  Hi, I'm hidden. Notice that all of my styling is hidden as well, and that I still take up space, even though you can't see me.
  <div class="visibility-visible border-width-1 border-color-black border-style-solid">
    Howdy, my parent element is hidden, but I'm still visible. <br>Hover over me to make my parent visible.
  </div>
</div>

SCSS

$visibility: ();
$visibility: map-merge(
  (
    "hidden": hidden,
    "collapse": collapse,
    "visible": visible,
  ),
  $visibility
);
